What is Special Education at SCGC?

At Sree Child Guidance Centre, our Special Education programme goes beyond conventional classroom learning. We create individualized education plans (IEPs) that are thoughtfully crafted to match every child's unique learning style, pace, and abilities.

Our certified special educators work hand-in-hand with therapists, parents, and caregivers to ensure a holistic, integrated approach that bridges academic learning with real-life functional skills — building independence, confidence, and joy in learning.

Whether your child is diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder, Down Syndrome, Intellectual Disability, ADHD, or any other developmental challenge — we are here to support their educational journey every step of the way.

Our Step-by-Step Approach

A structured, child-centered process that ensures every child receives the right support from day one.

1

🔍 Comprehensive Assessment

We begin with a thorough evaluation of your child's cognitive, communicative, social, and academic abilities — using standardized tools and direct observation to build a complete profile.

2

📋 Individualized Education Plan (IEP)

Based on assessment results, our special educators collaborate with therapists and parents to develop a personalized IEP with clear, measurable goals and a realistic timeline.

3

🎯 Targeted, Engaging Sessions

Sessions are conducted using evidence-based methods including Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA), Structured Teaching (TEACCH), and multi-sensory learning — adapted to each child's pace.

4

📊 Regular Progress Monitoring

We track each child's progress continuously and adjust the plan as needed — ensuring goals remain challenging yet achievable, and celebrating every milestone along the way.

5

🏠 Home & Community Carryover

We train parents and caregivers with practical strategies to reinforce learning at home and in the community — ensuring skills transfer beyond the therapy room.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions parents most often ask us about our Special Education programme.

What age group does your Special Education programme support? +
We support children from as young as 2 years old through adolescence. Early intervention is highly effective, but it is never too late to begin — our programmes are adapted to suit children at any stage of their developmental journey.
How is an IEP (Individualized Education Plan) created for my child? +
After a comprehensive initial assessment, our special educators, therapists, and parents collaborate to design a personalised plan. The IEP includes short-term and long-term goals, teaching strategies, resources needed, and regular review checkpoints to track progress.
How often will my child attend Special Education sessions? +
Session frequency is determined individually based on the child's needs, goals, and the recommendations of our team. Many children attend 3–5 sessions per week, and this can be combined with other therapies for maximum benefit.
Can Special Education be combined with other therapies at SCGC? +
Absolutely — and this is where SCGC truly excels. Our integrated model means your child's special educator works closely with our speech therapists, occupational therapists, and behavioral therapists to ensure a coordinated, holistic approach to development.
How will I know if my child is making progress? +
We provide regular progress reports and parent feedback meetings. Progress is measured against the IEP goals using data-driven tracking. You will always know exactly where your child stands and what the next steps look like.
Do you provide training to parents as well? +
Yes! We strongly believe parents are a child's most important educators. We provide structured parent training sessions that equip you with practical strategies to support your child's learning at home and reinforce the skills taught in sessions.
