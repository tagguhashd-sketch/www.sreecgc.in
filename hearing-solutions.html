What Are Pediatric Hearing Solutions?

Hearing is fundamental to a child's speech, language, social, and cognitive development. Our comprehensive hearing solutions program provides early detection, assessment, intervention, and support for children with hearing difficulties. We offer state-of-the-art audiological services combined with therapeutic support to maximize your child's hearing potential.

💡 Critical Window: The first 3 years of life are crucial for language development. Early identification and intervention for hearing loss can dramatically improve a child's communication skills, academic success, and quality of life. Even mild hearing loss can impact learning!

Our audiologists and hearing specialists work closely with families to provide complete hearing care—from screening and diagnosis to hearing aids, cochlear implant support, auditory training, and speech therapy for children with hearing impairment.

Signs of Hearing Difficulties in Children

👶

No Startle Response

Baby doesn't react to loud sounds or sudden noises

🔇

Doesn't Turn to Sounds

Not turning head toward voices or sounds by 6 months

🗣️

Speech Delays

Not babbling or speaking age-appropriate words

📢

Says "Huh?" or "What?"

Frequently asking for repetition or not responding when called

📺

Excessive Volume

Turns TV or devices very loud, speaks loudly

👀

Watches Faces Intently

Relies heavily on visual cues to understand speech

🎓

Academic Struggles

Difficulty following classroom instructions or learning

🤷

Unclear Speech

Difficulty pronouncing sounds, especially consonants

Types of Hearing Loss We Address

Our hearing solutions program effectively manages:

  • ✓ Conductive Hearing Loss (ear infections, fluid, structural issues)
  • ✓ Sensorineural Hearing Loss (inner ear or nerve damage)
  • ✓ Mixed Hearing Loss (combination of both types)
  • ✓ Auditory Processing Disorder (APD)
  • ✓ Congenital Hearing Loss (present from birth)
  • ✓ Progressive Hearing Loss
  • ✓ Unilateral Hearing Loss (one ear)
  • ✓ Temporary Hearing Issues (fluid, infections)

🎧 Advanced Technology: We use the latest audiological equipment including play audiometry for young children, otoacoustic emissions (OAE) testing, and advanced hearing aid technology specifically designed for pediatric use with safety features and durability!

Beyond Hearing Aids: Complete Support

We understand that hearing loss impacts more than just hearing. Our holistic approach includes:

  • ✓ Speech and Language Therapy for children with hearing impairment
  • ✓ Educational advocacy and school support planning
  • ✓ Parent training on communication strategies
  • ✓ Assistive listening devices for classroom use
  • ✓ Coordination with ENT specialists when needed
  • ✓ Social-emotional support for children and families
