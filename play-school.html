What Makes Our Play School Special?

Our specialized play school is designed exclusively for children with developmental delays, learning disabilities, and special needs. We provide a safe, structured, and inclusive learning environment where every child receives individualized attention and evidence-based educational interventions tailored to their unique abilities and learning style.

💡 Inclusive Education: We believe every child has the right to learn, grow, and reach their full potential. Our play school combines special education principles with therapeutic support to create a comprehensive early intervention program that prepares children for future academic and life success.

Unlike traditional preschools, our program integrates speech therapy, occupational therapy, behavioral therapy, and special education instruction throughout the day. Our specially trained teachers and therapists work as a team to support each child's developmental, academic, social, and emotional growth.

Structured routine with songs, calendar, weather, and social greetings to build predictability and social skills

2

What We Teach

Our comprehensive curriculum covers all developmental domains:

  • ✓ Pre-Academic Skills: Colors, shapes, numbers, letters, sorting, matching
  • ✓ Communication Skills: Expressive & receptive language, social communication
  • ✓ Fine Motor Skills: Pre-writing, coloring, cutting, manipulating objects
  • ✓ Gross Motor Skills: Balance, coordination, body awareness, strength
  • ✓ Social Skills: Sharing, turn-taking, following group rules, making friends
  • ✓ Self-Care Skills: Toileting, eating, dressing, hygiene routines
  • ✓ Behavioral Regulation: Following directions, waiting, managing emotions
  • ✓ Play Skills: Imaginative play, cooperative play, appropriate toy use

Parent Partnership

We believe parents are essential partners in their child's education:

  • ✓ Daily communication logs and progress updates
  • ✓ Regular parent-teacher conferences and goal reviews
  • ✓ Parent training workshops on home strategies
  • ✓ Home activity suggestions to reinforce learning
  • ✓ Open-door policy for observations and questions
  • ✓ Family support and resource connections

🎓 School Readiness: Our ultimate goal is to prepare children for successful transition to inclusive or specialized school settings. We work on pre-academic skills, classroom behaviors, and independence to set a strong foundation for future learning!
